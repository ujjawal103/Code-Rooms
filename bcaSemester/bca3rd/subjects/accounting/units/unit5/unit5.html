Financial Management Overview

This section includes the meaning, nature, and approaches to financial management. It also covers the objectives of financial management, focusing on Profit Maximization Objective (PMO) vs. Wealth Maximization Objective (WMO). Additionally, it discusses financial decisions, such as financing, investment, dividend, and liquidity decisions, and explores the liquidity vs. profitability trade-off.

Time Value of Money and Sources of Finance

This section covers the time value of money, including basic valuation concepts using compounding and discounting techniques. It also provides a brief overview of short-term and long-term sources of finance, their features, and their importance in financial planning.
